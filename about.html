About Us

Welcome to Govinda's Prasadam

Govinda's Prasadam is a divine initiative by ISKCON Hadapsar, dedicated to serving sanctified, Satvik, and pure vegetarian meals prepared with deep devotion and hygiene.

Our kitchen follows strict spiritual and safety standards. We believe that food is not just about filling the stomach but nourishing the soul. Every grain served is first offered to the Lord, making it 'Prasadam' (Sanctified Mercy).

Our Mission

To distribute sanctified, healthy, and karma-free food to society, promoting a lifestyle of compassion and higher consciousness.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% Pure Vegetarian: No meat, fish, or eggs.
  • No Onion, No Garlic: Strictly following Satvik guidelines.
  • Hygiene First: Prepared in a clean, modern kitchen facility.
  • Spiritual Vibration: Cooked while chanting holy names.
