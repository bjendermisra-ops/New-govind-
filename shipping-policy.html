Shipping & Delivery

Service Area

We currently deliver exclusively in Hadapsar, Pune and surrounding areas within a 5-7 km radius of ISKCON Hadapsar.

Delivery Timing

Standard Delivery: Orders are typically delivered within 45-60 minutes of order confirmation.

Peak Hours: During festivals or weekends, delivery may take up to 90 minutes. We will keep you informed via live tracking/phone.

Shipping Costs

Delivery charges are calculated based on distance:

  • 0-2 km: ₹20
  • 2-5 km: ₹40
  • Orders above ₹500: FREE Delivery
