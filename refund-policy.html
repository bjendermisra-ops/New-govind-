Refund & Cancellation

At Govinda's Prasadam, we strive to serve you the best quality sanctified food. However, due to the perishable nature of our products, we have specific policies regarding cancellations and refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

As our food is prepared fresh against every order, we have a strict cancellation policy:

  • Within 5 Minutes: You can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it by calling our support number.
  • After Preparation Starts: Once the kitchen begins preparation (Order Status: "Cooking"), cancellations are not accepted.

2. Refunds

We do not offer refunds for delivered food items unless:

  • The order was missing items.
  • The packaging was severely damaged/spilled upon arrival.
  • The wrong item was delivered.

In such cases, please contact us immediately with photos. Upon verification, we will initiate a partial or full refund.

Refund Timeline: Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment source within 5-7 business days.

3. Payment Failures

If an amount is deducted from your account but the order is not placed (Order Failed), the amount will be automatically refunded by the gateway within 5-7 working days.
